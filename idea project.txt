Your challenge is to build a working MVP of an AI-powered itinerary planner that helps users plan trips efficiently. Participants can leverage AI technologies (like GPT, travel APIs, recommendation systems, etc.) to create a functional tool.

MVP Requirements:

Must allow users to input destinations, dates, and preferences.
Must generate a working itinerary (day-wise schedule, recommended places to visit, activities, and optional notes).
Can integrate AI suggestions for optimization or personalization.
Should be demonstrable as a working prototype by the end of the hackathon.
Optional Enhancements:

Map-based visualizations.
Cost estimation.
Integration with booking platforms.
Eligibility:

Open to students and professionals interested in AI, travel, and product development.
